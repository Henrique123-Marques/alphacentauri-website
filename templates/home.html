Alpha Centauri System

Alpha Centauri is the closest stellar and
planetary system to our Solar System, being "only"
a little over 4.3 light-years away from Earth.
The system consists of the stars Alpha Centauri A, B and C.

A distance in light years represents that if we were able to reach the speed of light with some kind of vehicle, it would take a little more than 4 Earth years and 2 months to reach this trinary star system, below we have a schematic image of the Alpha Centauri system and also from Barnard's Star, the fourth closest star to us.
